## 4. Data Collection

### Server Log Files

Our web server automatically logs information when you visit the website, including:

- IP address
- Date and time of access
- Pages visited
- Referrer URL
- Browser type and version

These logs are retained for security and performance analysis. Legal basis: Legitimate interest (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR).

### Contact Forms

If you submit contact information through a form, we collect:

- Name
- Email address
- Message content

Legal basis: Consent (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR) and contract performance (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR).

## 5. Cookies

This website does **not** use tracking cookies or analytics cookies that require consent. We do not use cookies for marketing purposes.

## 6. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal data with third parties unless:

- You explicitly consent
- Required by law

## 7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes stated in this policy or as required by law. Contact form submissions are retained for reasonable time periods to respond to inquiries.

## 8. Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights:

- **Right of Access** (Art. 15 GDPR): Request access to your personal data
- **Right to Rectification** (Art. 16 GDPR): Correct inaccurate data
- **Right to Erasure** (Art. 17 GDPR): Request deletion of your data
- **Right to Restrict Processing** (Art. 18 GDPR)
- **Right to Data Portability** (Art. 20 GDPR): Receive your data in a portable format
- **Right to Object** (Art. 21 GDPR)
- **Right to Withdraw Consent** at any time

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the social media links or email provided in the footer of our website.

## 9. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

## 10. Contact & Data Protection Officer

For questions about this privacy policy or to exercise your rights, please contact us through the social media links in the footer of our website.

If you believe we have violated GDPR requirements, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent data protection authority (in Germany: your state's Landesbeauftragte für Datenschutz).
